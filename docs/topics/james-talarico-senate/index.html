James Talarico: Can He Win?

Texas State Rep. James Talarico (D-District 50) is running for U.S. Senate in Texas. The Democratic primary is today, March 3, 2026, with his main opponent Rep. Jasmine Crockett. Polls show a tight race with Talarico holding a narrow lead.

📊 The Bottom Line

Polls lean Talarico. YouGov (Mar 2): 53% vs 40%. Emerson (Mar 1): 52% vs 47% (within MOE). He leads among Hispanic, white, and younger voters. Crockett dominates Black voters (80-84%). Primary results tonight after 7 PM CT.
