James Talarico: Can He Win?

Texas State Rep. James Talarico (D-District 50) is running for U.S. Senate in Texas. The Democratic primary is today, March 3, 2026, with his main opponent Rep. Jasmine Crockett. Polls show a tight race with Talarico holding a narrow lead.

politics texas senate-2026 democratic-primary election

📊 The Bottom Line

Polls lean Talarico. YouGov (Mar 2): 53% vs 40%. Emerson (Mar 1): 52% vs 47% (within MOE). He leads among Hispanic, white, and younger voters. Crockett dominates Black voters (80-84%). Primary results tonight after 7 PM CT.

Key Facts

What you need to know about James Talarico

🏛️

Texas House Rep, District 50

Currently serving in the Texas Legislature. Former public school teacher turned politician.

🎓

Former Teacher

Background in education shapes his policy focus on affordability and working families.

💰

Economic Platform

Focuses on lowering cost of housing, childcare, and prescription drugs. Anti-billionaire messaging.

📺

Colbert Controversy

National attention after a canceled "Late Show" interview with Stephen Colbert became a major story.

🗳️

Strong Early Voting

Emerson poll shows 58% to 41% lead among early voters — a significant advantage heading in.

🤝

Coalition

Leads among Hispanic (60%), white (69-71%), and young voters (18-29: 60%). Crockett leads Black voters (80-84%).
